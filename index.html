What is MortalTiger

This project created by several members who are the best in their work. On the first stage, we want to give your attention on Tiger which is in Red Book. They lived in East Asia and we want to save them in NFT World. Our Designer created unique 2D Models of Tiger who are looks like fighter of Muay-Thai martial art. First collection will consist of 5555 NFT's. NFT's will peresented for minting as soon as possible. The place of launch will be anounced. In the first stage holders will be able to stake our Art NFT Collection and new 3D Collection in GIF format will be created as a second collection. Holders of the first collection will get GIF NFTs for free and will play our Game which will be presented for community. P2E Multiplayer Game will be created on Unity and will be similar to famous Mortal Kombar or Taken. Monthly tournaments will give players possibility to get different type of gifts.
